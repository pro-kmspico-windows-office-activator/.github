## 📋 Under the Hood
### 🔬 **Technology Explained**
The tool works by mimicking an official KMS server on your computer. This lets your Windows and Office products activate seamlessly, as if they were on a business network—simplifying what is usually a complex process! 🧠

- **Bit Versions:** Fully supports both 32-bit and 64-bit systems
